Places You Should Visit at Malang

Kebun Apel Malang

Good place to start your tour in Malang. Here, you can experience picking, planting, caring for, and also enjoying fresh Malang apples.



Taman Langit Gunung Banyak

Must visit if you like to take pictures with beauty. Here, there are many natural scenery that can be used as photo spots. Not only that, there are also many artificial photo spots that provide a photo experience in the sky garden.



Taman Kelinci Pujon

Heaven for rabbits. Here you can play freely with the rabbits. You can also feed the rabbits here.
