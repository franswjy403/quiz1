About Malang

Malang, The Flower City

Malang, such a beautiful city with a long history. The hallmarks of this city are the cool air, beautiful scenery, and its friendly people. Not only that, Malang is also known as the producer of very intelligent students. Therefore, Malang is often referred to as the city of education.
